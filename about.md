# Creator Studio

A level creation toolkit for creators who want to build rated-worthy levels.

## Three panels, accessible from buttons on the right side of the editor:

### Anim — Animation Studio
Select objects → pick an animation → triggers are auto-generated. No manual trigger chaining needed.

**13 presets across 4 categories:**
- **Motion**: Float, Pulse Scale, Spin, Shake, Orbit
- **Entrance**: Slide In Left/Right, Drop In, Fade In/Out
- **Pulse**: Color Flash
- **Deco**: Parallax Drift, Twinkle

Each preset auto-assigns a group and places the trigger chain below your objects.

### Color — Color Palettes
Browse 12 pre-built color schemes inspired by rated level styles.

Palettes include: Abyss (dark), Neon Rush, Sunset, Forest, Arctic, Inferno, Void (minimal), Sakura (pastel), Cyber (tech), Galaxy (space), Bloodbath (hell), Ocean.

Each palette shows 6 colors (BG, ground, primary, accent, detail, text) with RGB values you can manually apply to your color channels.

### Check — Level Analyzer
Scans your entire level and gives you a report:
- Object, trigger, hazard, and decoration counts
- Gameplay, decoration, and trigger scores (0-100)
- Estimated difficulty rating
- Issue detection (empty sections, low decoration, missing variety)
- Tips for getting your level rated

Also available from the editor pause menu.

## Tips for getting rated

1. **Decoration ratio > 50%** — More decoration objects than gameplay objects
2. **1000+ total objects** — Substantive, polished levels
3. **50+ triggers** — Color transitions, pulse effects, move triggers
4. **Multiple game modes** — Ship, wave, ball sections add variety
5. **Consistent difficulty** — Avoid huge spikes between sections
6. **Music sync** — Pulse/color triggers on the beat
